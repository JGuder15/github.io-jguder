About Me

about

My name is Jason Guderjahn and I am excited to join the professional technical communication environment.

Having just graduated from Iowa State, I am excited to apply my gathered knowledge and skills to the professional atmosphere of technical communication. At Iowa State, I majored in technical communication, with a minor in Linguistics.

My Home Life

I was born and raised in Minnesota, althought I am excited to travel around as I broaden my horizons, both professionally and personally.

I grew up in Rosemount, MN, which is where I spent all of my time up until I went off to college. I excelled academically, although some medical concerns created a tumultuous end to my high school career. I had to attend night classes to graduate on time, and I still managed to graduate near the top of my class. From there, I was accepted by Iowa State University, where I initially went to study engineering. While that coursework did not intrigue me, I was able to find technical communication, which melded the problem solving nature of engineering with the writing aspects that I have always loved. In my free time, I enjoy spending time outside, writing, reading, and expanding my horizons overall. Just as I dedicate myself to my work, I bring the same zeal and enthusiasm to my hobbies as well.

My Work Life

I am a motivated, dedicated, and punctual individual, and these traits translate exceptionally well into my professional life.

One primary attribute that I am especially interested in pursuing is that of social justice in technical communication. My coursework at Iowa State and the professional experience that I have gathered there have emphasized the importance of inclusion, diversity, and justice. As I enter the professional world, I intend to cement these ideas into my work.
